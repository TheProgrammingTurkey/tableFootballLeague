Controls

With the mouse, touchpad, or touchscreen, swipe across the football to hit it.

Instructions

Every game starts with a kickoff. Swipe up or down on the football towards your opponents end.

For every hit, your goal is to hit the football so that it is partially hanging off your opponents end of the table (A Touchdown).

If the ball happens to slide off the table entirely, that is called an "Off" (The second number on the scoreboard).

If one team gets three offs before either team scores, the opposing team gets a field goal.

For field goals, your goal is to hit the ball through the uprights.

The game is first to 30 points.

Scoring

Touchdown (6): Hit the football so that it is partially hanging off your opponents end of the table

Extra Point (1): Hit the football through the uprights after the touchdown

Two Point Conversion (2): Hit the football so that it is partially hanging off your opponents end of the table after the touchdown

Field Goal (3): Hit the football through the uprights after 3 Offs from the opponent

Safety (2*): Hit the football partially off, or entirely off of your own side (* 2 points for the opponent)
